Overview of Trevor Something

Trevor Something is an independent music producer from Miami, Florida. He has been creating music since 2011, and his music is mostly within the re-imerging synthwave genra. In recent years he has started to produce more grunge/punk related songs.

Trevor Somethings most popular album is Death Dream. Released in October of 2015, it is an immersive trip into a world of harsh synthesizers and medlodic lyrics.
